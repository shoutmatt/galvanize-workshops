Have Fun!

Learning a new skill is rarely easy. The key is finding ways to have own your own fun working on it and not letting yourself get discouraged when things don't work out right away. Students in the past that got the most out of these workshops tended to have personal projects, or even projects for others, to keep them inspired. If you have problems knowing what to do, reach out to me in Slack, Matthew Leach, and I'll be happy to help.
